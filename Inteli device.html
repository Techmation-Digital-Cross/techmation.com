Introduction to iNteli

WHAT IS iNteli?

iNteli is a smart IOT device made in Myanmar (Burma) which can control Aircon and TV .

WHY YOU NEED IT?

The answer to this question is simple . It enables the lifestyles of customers easier and more convenient . In addition, it can be controlled with the tip of your finger and therefore reduces energy consuming with higher efficiency .

HOW iNteli WORKS?

iNteli uses smart IR sensor which senses room temperature and manages aircon power using effectiven cost reducing algorithms.It is deployed on cloud and it can be fully controlled via mobile application .

Advantages of using iNteli

  • Reduce energy consuming
  • Easy to access
  • Centralized contol from one user
  • Automation system
